The Carbon Milk Run · Centerpiece

This is how
every industry connects.

Small industries plug in through micro-capture units at each source. Milk-run trucks aggregate CO₂ into the central Carbon Hub, powered by solar and wind, running three processing bays. Outputs fan out below as four revenue streams. Credit revenue closes the loop.

◈ Spoke · Micro-Capture
Modular capture units at foundries, kilns, ceramics, textiles, cement. Each stores CO₂ in cylinders.
→ Logistics · Milk Run
Smart-routed shared trucks collect filled cylinders on scheduled loops across the industrial ring.
◉ Hub · Processing
Central facility runs capture → liquefaction → extrusion, powered by solar + wind, with buffer tank.
↓ Outputs · Revenue
Four parallel streams: Specialty PCC, mineralization aggregates, CCTS/VCM credits, future fuels & chemicals.
Our First Commercial Product

Start where the pain is greatest.
Small & mid industrial emitters.

India alone has 200,000+ small and mid industrial units that emit CO₂ but cannot afford dedicated carbon infrastructure. We start there — the market no one else serves.

Carbon Milk Run System · SME Emitters
Micro-Capture + Shared Hub for Small & Medium Industries

Problem

An SME emitting 2–50 tCO₂/day cannot justify a ₹10 Cr capture unit. They face rising carbon regulations, no compliance path, no monetization option, and no infrastructure partner. They emit and pray.

Solution

A ₹15–25 lakh modular micro-capture unit installed on-site. Filled CO₂ cylinders picked up on scheduled milk-run routes. Aggregated at the Carbon Hub for conversion into PCC, pellets, and credits.

Customer Profile

  • Small foundries (2–20 tCO₂/day)
  • Ceramics & brick kilns
  • Textile dyeing units
  • Mid-tier cement grinding plants
  • Food processing operations

Revenue Model

  • Micro-capture unit lease / EMI
  • Per-tonne capture-as-a-service fee
  • Revenue share on BEE credits generated
  • Downstream product sales (PCC, pellets)

Proprietary vs Integrated

Proprietary: the aggregation network, the routing OS, the credit stack, the SME onboarding playbook.
Integrated: capture chemistry, liquefaction, credit registries — sourced from proven third parties.

First Revenue Path

Sign 8–12 anchor SMEs in a single industrial cluster (Morbi ceramics, Rajkot foundries, Bhilwara textiles). Commission Hub v0.1. First revenue: capture-service fees, month 3. First credits monetized, month 12.

Dual Carbon Pathway

Not every molecule
should become a product.

Some carbon should be permanently stored to maximize climate impact and long-term credit value. Carbon Chakra runs both pathways — the routing OS decides which molecule goes where.

Pathway 01 · Utilization
Carbon becomes product.
For streams where market value exceeds sequestration credit price, carbon is converted into revenue-generating molecules.
  • Methanol & derivativesCATALYTIC
  • Specialty PCCMINERAL
  • Construction aggregatesCARBONATION
  • Food-grade CO₂PURIFIED
  • Industrial feedstocksSYNTHESIS
Pathway 02 · Permanent Storage
Carbon becomes climate impact.
For streams where high-integrity credits outperform product economics — or where products aren't yet mature — carbon is sequestered permanently.
  • Geological injectionDEEP · SALINE
  • Basalt mineralizationGEOCHEMICAL
  • Ultramafic tailingsMINE SITE
  • Ocean alkalinity enhancementEMERGING
  • Long-cycle biocharBIO-BASED
